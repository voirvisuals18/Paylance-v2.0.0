# Paylance

**Your Personal Salary & Budget Companion.**  
Paylance is a sleek, offline-first application designed to give you absolute clarity over your monthly salary, budget allocations, and savings goals.

## Premium Features

- **Dashboard Clarity**: Instantly view your total balance, monthly income, and spending at a glance.
- **Automated Budgeting**: Intelligent 50/30/20 rule allocation for Needs, Wants, and Savings.
- **Smart Tracking**: Beautifully categorized expense history.
- **Progress Tracking**: Dynamic progress bars for all your financial goals.
- **Insightful Analytics**: 6-month trend charts and category breakdown visualizations.
- **Total Personalization**: Dark and Light themes with multiple elegant accent colors.
- **Data Sovereignty**: 100% offline. Your financial data never leaves your device.
- **Secure Backups**: Easy JSON export and restore functionality.

## How to Use
1. **Initial Setup**: Enter your monthly salary and recurring expenses.
2. **Track**: Add your daily spending under tailored categories.
3. **Save**: Set savings goals and watch your progress grow.
4. **Optimize**: Use the Reports screen to adjust your spending habits.

## Privacy
Paylance respects your privacy. No data is collected, tracked, or shared. All information is stored locally on your device.
